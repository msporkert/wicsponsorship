Women in Computing @ UCSD

We're coders and engineers - both women and men – who support and encourage the female presence in computing. We run events, from programming competitions to tech talks & socials, and provide opportunities for female engineers to connect and succeed.

who are we

our mission

Our mission is to foster an innovative and informative environment for individuals in computer science, increase the enrollment, spark interest, and increase the retention rate of women in computer science, and progress to a time where the topic of gender or minority groups is trivial and everyone is judged based on their intelligence and capacity rather than their gender
